Ovarian Cancer
Subtype Classification

Ovarian cancer is a type of cancer that originates in the ovaries, the female reproductive organs responsible for producing eggs and hormones. Early detection remains challenging, contributing to the generally poorer prognosis associated with ovarian cancer. Deep learning and machine learning models are increasingly being employed in the field of medical imaging to assist in the diagnosis of diseases from images of tissues. In the context of tissue diagnosis, convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and pre-trained models like ResNet,VGG have shown remarkable efficacy. These deep learning models can automatically extract hierarchical features from images, enabling them to identify subtle patterns indicative of various tissue abnormalities. The goal of this project is to greatly increase ovarian cancer therapy efficacy, accessibility, and diagnostic accuracy. The project's potential to lessen negative effects and improve patient outcomes is highlighted by the employment of machine learning and deep learning techniques.
